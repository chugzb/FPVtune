## General Questions

### What is FPVTune?

FPVTune is an AI-powered tuning assistant that analyzes your drone's flight data and provides optimized PID settings. Simply upload your Blackbox logs or CLI dump, and our AI will identify issues and suggest improvements.

### How does it work?

Our AI has been trained on thousands of flight logs from various drones and flying styles. When you upload your data, it analyzes patterns, identifies issues like oscillations or propwash, and generates optimized settings tailored to your specific setup.

### Is FPVTune free?

We offer both free and premium plans. The free plan includes basic analysis and recommendations. Premium plans provide advanced features like detailed reports, historical tracking, and priority support.

## Using FPVTune

### What files do I need to upload?

You can upload:
- **Blackbox logs** (.bbl, .bfl files) - Contains detailed flight data
- **CLI dump** (.txt files) - Contains your current configuration

For best results, upload both files together.

### How long does analysis take?

Most analyses complete within 10-30 seconds. Complex logs or high traffic periods may take slightly longer.

### What firmware versions are supported?

We support:
- Betaflight 4.0 and newer
- INAV 2.0 and newer
- EmuFlight
- Other Betaflight-based firmware

### Can I use FPVTune for racing drones?

Yes! FPVTune works with all types of FPV drones including racing quads, freestyle builds, and cinematic rigs. Our AI adapts recommendations based on your flying style.

## Account & Subscription

### Do I need an account?

A free account is required to use FPVTune. This allows us to save your analysis history and provide personalized recommendations.

### How do I upgrade to premium?

Visit the [pricing page](/pricing) to view available plans and upgrade options. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time.

### Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain premium access until the end of your billing period.

### What payment methods do you accept?

## Technical Support

### My analysis failed. What should I do?

First, ensure your log files are valid and not corrupted. If the problem persists, contact our support team with details about the error.

### The recommended settings made my drone worse

Every drone is unique. Start by applying 50-70% of the recommended changes, test fly, and adjust gradually. If issues persist, contact support for personalized help.

### Can I share my analysis results?

Yes! Each analysis has a unique shareable link. You can share this with friends or the community to get additional feedback.

### How do I export my settings?

After analysis, you can download the recommended settings as a CLI dump file or copy individual parameters to paste into Betaflight Configurator.

## Privacy & Security

### Is my flight data private?

Yes. Your uploaded files and analysis results are private by default. We only use anonymized data to improve our AI models, and you can opt out anytime.

### Do you sell my data?

Never. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or flight data with third parties.

### How long do you keep my files?

Uploaded files are stored for 30 days, after which they are automatically deleted. Premium users can choose to keep files longer.
